Research Vector

My work lives where astrostatistics, machine learning and leaked / declassified archives collide. I design pipelines that treat sky surveys and intelligence dumps as one continuous, messy dataset – then ask: does anything in here look more like intent than noise?

Signal mining
Anomalous sky patterns

Deep anomaly models on radio and optical surveys, hunting for persistent non-terrestrial signatures across time, frequency and instrument.

Classified echoes
Declassified & leaked archives

Topic models and sequence tagging over FOIA releases, historic intelligence reports and incident logs, looking for recurring non-human craft narratives.

Consistency checks
Cross-domain alignment

If the same latent anomaly shows up in sky data and classified documents, either the universe is trolling us – or someone isn’t telling the full story.
